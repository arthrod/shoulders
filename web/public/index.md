## AI Integration -- Work with AI on a shared canvas

The AI can access your workspace and help you write or find references -- it can suggest changes directly in your document.

## References -- Surprisingly great reference management

Add references by pasting titles, DOIs, or dropping PDFs -- Shoulders will try to find and verify the metadata.

## Writing -- Built to augment, not intrude

The AI stays out of your way until you ask -- press ++ and get a few ideas to complete your thought without breaking your flow.

## Coding and Data -- Analysis and manuscript in one place

Run code next to your draft. The AI can critique your analysis, or check that your methods section actually matches your code.

## Features

### Full control

All files stay on your machine. You can use API keys to connect to public or private LLMs -- or sign up for simplicity.

### Shared context

Your references, notes, PDFs, data, code, and manuscripts -- all in one workspace, shared with your AI research assistant.

### Write in any format

Word, Markdown, or LaTeX. Export publication-ready documents with formatted citations.

### Linked notes

Connect ideas with wiki links. Bidirectional references between your notes and papers.

### Full transparency

Shoulders' full source code is openly available. No hidden fees. No vendor lock-in.

### Version control

Every change saved automatically. Full history, one-click restore. Never lose work again.
